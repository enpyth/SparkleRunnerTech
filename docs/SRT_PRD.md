## Core Business 1

SparkleRunnerTech, headquartered in Adelaide, South Australia, is an AgeTech company that publicly offers a clinical-grade AI companionship system, addressing four major pain points faced by Australia's 4.7 million elderly people living at home: loneliness, cognitive health, healthcare shortage, and intergenerational communication.

"Smart Clinical Guardian, Empowering Every Elderly Australian to Lead an Independent Life with a Touch of Warmth"

Product positioning: big picture is a prototype of a clinical-grade interactive terminal
Introduction to functional sections:
- Digital Twin Conversational Analysis System (emotional and psychological state tracking, cognitive function monitoring, pain expression analysis, family social relationship quality assessment) complies with the RACGP digital health standards
- Family communication advice and tips (recommending communication topics based on the patient's interest response and cognitive status, as well as expressing the patient's emotional needs)
- Health data analysis and early warning system (seamlessly connecting the monitoring infrastructure of existing hospitals and elderly care institutions, generating EMR based on vital sign monitoring devices and dialogue content signals to assist in clinical early warning)

Optional value-added services:
- Clinical Customisation
- Customize risk models based on the patient structure of the institution (dementia/depression/falls/delirium)
- Cultural and ethnic sensitivity tuning for local language (CALD population)
- Nursing process mapping and AI parameter calibration
- Family Plus
- In-depth report on emotional support and communication strategies for family members
- Assessment of family stress and caregiving burden
- Cross-regional family collaboration module
- Caregiving conflicts among overseas children/families living apart/families with multiple children
- Hierarchical permissions for family members
- Conflict early warning (uneven caregiving responsibilities)
- Synchronized reminders for important medical checkpoints
